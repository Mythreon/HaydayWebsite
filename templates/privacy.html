Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 29, 2025

At HayDay 🍀, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights as a user of this site.

🔍 What We Collect

  • Your Discord ID and username when you log in
  • Your IP address (used for abuse protection and rate limiting)
  • Your browser’s user agent string
  • Interaction logs (e.g., button clicks, link visits)

📌 Why We Collect This

This data is collected to:

  • Enable Discord login
  • Prevent scams, bots, and fraudulent behavior
  • Block suspicious activity like website spam or DDoS attacks
  • Improve site performance and debug errors

🛡 How We Use and Store Your Data

All data is securely stored in a private database. Only trusted staff have access. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

IP addresses and interaction logs are used strictly for anti-abuse. Logs are auto-deleted after 30 days.

🔒 How We Detect and Block Abuse (Click to expand)

To protect the HayDay 🍀 website and community from malicious activity, we use automated protections that monitor incoming traffic for abuse.

  • 🚫 Block IPs that repeatedly access suspicious or invalid paths (e.g., WordPress files)
  • ⏱ Rate limit excessive requests to avoid server overload
  • 🛡️ Auto-ban IPs after 20+ abuse triggers within a short period

These systems help defend against spam, bots, and DDoS attempts. All abuse bans are temporary (7 days) and stored securely. Only trusted staff can view or clear them.

⚖️ Your Rights

You may request access to your data or ask for it to be deleted at any time. Please contact the site owner or staff via Discord.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We will notify users through the website or our Discord server if any significant changes are made to this policy.

📬 Contact

For questions or data requests, contact us through our Discord server or message XavierDK.
